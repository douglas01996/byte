Quality: About This Data Set

The dataset is trustworthy but there might be some mistakes as shown in the following part.

Is the data complete?

The data is appropriate as it match the questions I want to answer. There are many missing data because sometimes AWARE cannot determine the name of locations or the activities I perform. There are reporting errors. For example, it detects sometimes I was cycling which I never did.

Is the data coherent?

The data is coherent. As we could see from the visualization above, the locations make sense as well as the activities.

Is the data correct?

I examine the data and I think the recorded data are correct except for the activities. Sometimes I would walk around the neighborhoods and leave my phone at home.
